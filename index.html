Nigeria

Nigeria, in West Africa, is the continent's most populous nation, rich in diverse cultures, languages, and traditions. Home to over 250 ethnic groups, it’s known for its vibrant music, art, and the booming Nollywood film industry. Major oil production drives its economy, alongside growth in agriculture and services. With bustling cities like Lagos and landmarks like Zuma Rock, Nigeria’s energy and natural beauty shine, and its people are known for resilience, warmth, and influence worldwide.
